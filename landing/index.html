scroll0

The only Chrome extension that makes you verbally admit you're wasting time before letting you in.

scroll0 blocks your most addictive websites and forces you to confess your intent. If you want to waste 15 minutes of your life, you have to say it out loud.

Install for Chrome

  1. Clone the GitHub repository.
  2. Open chrome://extensions in your browser.
  3. Enable Developer mode in the top right.
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the scroll0 folder.
